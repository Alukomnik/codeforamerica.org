How we work with governments

Fellowship

Code for America partners with local governments, to build capabilities that help cities embrace a culture of innovative problem-solving while leveraging technology and data to address community needs. Cities who have partnered with us have built apps that support issues such as criminal justice, economic development and public health.

Network

Are you a forward-thinking government employee? Join our national network of public sector innovators. Network members are working at all levels of municipal government to advance 21st century solutions to local challenges, helping to make government more effective, efficient and responsive.

Communities

The Code for America Brigade program is an international network of people committed to using their voices and hands, in collaboration with local governments, to make their cities better. Brigades build participatory power in their cities by holding regular civic hack nights and events, advocating for open data, and deploying apps.
